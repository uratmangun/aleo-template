## Build Privacy-Preserving Apps Across Every Vertical

Aleo's zero-knowledge architecture enables privacy in any application. Here are high-impact verticals where your innovation can shine:

### 💰 Private Finance (DeFi)

- #### Private Prediction Markets

- #### Private Trading & Liquidity
  - **Shielded AMMs and DEXs** — Positions, balances, and order sizes stay confidential.
  - **Private Limit Orders, TWAP, or RFQ Systems** — Eliminate MEV and front-running.
  - **Smart Routers** — Keep order routing logic and execution details hidden.
  - **Dark Pools** — Institutional-grade privacy on large trades.

- #### Privacy-Preserving Credit & Lending
  - **Undercollateralized Loans** — Borrowers prove creditworthiness via ZK proofs.
  - **Private Onchain Credit Scores** — Verifiable but not fully transparent.
  - **ZK-based Eligibility Checks** — KYC, jurisdiction, and whitelisting without exposing raw data.

- #### Cross-Chain Private Yield & Structured Products
  - **Private Yield Vaults** — Fueled by liquidity bridged from other chains.
  - **Exotic DeFi Products** — Options, tranches, rate products where individual positions remain private but system solvency is provable.
  - **Strategy Aggregators** — Hide per-user allocations while proving fair execution.

- #### Institutional & Treasury DeFi
  - **Treasury Tools** — For funds, DAOs, and companies to keep strategies private while offering ZK proofs for compliance.
  - **Private Payroll and B2B Payment Rails** — Selective disclosure for auditors.
  - **DeFi Rails for Regulated Entities** — For those who cannot use fully transparent chains.

### 🆔 Private Identity & Credentials

- **Identity Verification** — Prove age, citizenship, or credentials without revealing documents.
- **Private KYC/AML** — Compliance without data exposure.
- **Anonymous Reputation Systems** — Build trust without doxxing.
- **Credential Wallets** — Store and selectively disclose certifications.

### 🗳️ Private Governance & Voting

- **Anonymous Voting** — Vote without revealing your choice.
- **Private DAOs** — Governance where member holdings stay confidential.
- **Whistleblower Systems** — Report safely with cryptographic protection.
- **Private Polls and Surveys** — Gather honest data without surveillance.

### 🧠 Privacy-Preserving AI & Data

- **Private ML Inference** — Run AI models on encrypted data.
- **Confidential Data Marketplaces** — Sell data without exposing it.
- **Private Analytics** — Aggregate insights without individual tracking.
- **Secure MPC Protocols** — Collaborate without sharing raw data.

### 🌐 Private Social & Communication

- **Anonymous Social Networks** — Express freely without surveillance.
- **Private Messaging** — Verify sender without revealing identity.
- **Confidential Content Platforms** — Monetize without exposure.
- **Private Professional Networks** — Network without revealing employer data.

## Rules

### 1. One submission per person/team
You may not submit multiple projects, either individually or as a part of a team or multiple teams. Those who attempt to subvert this rule via creating multiple GitHub accounts or similar methods will be detected and banned from future consideration in the Buildathon.

### 2. Projects must have a functional frontend and non-trivial Leo code deployed on at least Aleo Testnet
In order to better respect the judges' time, your project must have a live frontend that can be tested. In particular, there must be a way from your frontend to perform a non-trivial interaction with your contract deployed on Testnet/Mainnet. Additionally, your on-chain program must be something other than the default program created by `leo new`.

### 3. Projects must provide a descriptive and concise update through the Akindo platform
Even if you're submitting for the first time, provide a concise description of what you worked on this wave, and specify which features you haven't finished yet so judges have context.

### 4. Projects must take advantage of Aleo's core tooling
If your project uses payments, you must integrate and utilize the types and methods in either the native `credits.aleo`, or the USDCx or USAD stablecoin programs. Furthermore, all projects are required to integrate **[Shield Wallet](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/hhddpjpacfjaakjioinajgmhlbhfchao?utm_source=hackathon&utm_medium=mainpage&utm_campaign=akindo)** at minimum.

### 5. No AI Slop
We reserve the right to disqualify any projects that appear to be low-effort, mostly AI-generated submissions.

> Note: this does not mean that you can't use AI. Tools like Claude Code and GPT-Codex can be useful for accelerating development. However, low-effort submissions without serious depth or intention are not accepted.
